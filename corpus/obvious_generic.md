# obvious_generic.md

A short corpus of the most common, highest-probability "obvious" responses
that any average responder would produce for a generic creative brief.

Used by scripts/originality.py to anchor the originality score: a candidate
that closely resembles any of these entries scores low on originality.

Each line is one framing. Keep ≤ 2 sentences each.
